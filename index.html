What's included in a house cleaning?

We have a number of house cleaning services. One off deep clean or a regular housing keeping, contact us now to arrange a booking.

Bathroom cleaning services

We clean all your utilities including your baths, sinks, showers (including hair traps). Toilets cleaned inside and out, taps and chrome ware polished.

Checklist:
  • Bins emptied and big bags replaced.
  • Dusting, removing cobwebs from ceiling, light fixtures, wall hangings, all surfaces.
  • Shower cubicals including shower doors, drip trays, plugs and shower unites are cleaned.
  • Bathtubs scrubbed to remove grime.
  • Sinks cleaned insid and out.
  • Taps cleaned and polished in both bathtub and wash basin.
  • Toilet bowl and seat inside and out.
  • Mirrors and cabinets wiped, dusted and polished.
  • Blinds wiped down to remove any dust.
  • Rugs vacuumed, floor mopped.

Kitchen cleaning services

Your kitchen work surfaces cleaned, cupboard fronts, hob and white goods appliances all wiped down and polished.

Checklist:
  • Bins emptied and big bags replaced.
  • Dusting, removing cobwebs from ceiling, light fixtures, wall hangings, all surfaces.
  • Cleaning and polishing countertops and cupboards.
  • Cleaning oven, microwave, fridge and other appliances on the outside.
  • Sink, taps cleaned.
  • Blinds wiped down to remove any dust.
  • Sweeping, hoovering and mopping the floors.

Bedroom cleaning service

We tidy and clean your bedroom including making your beds.

Checklist:
  • Wastebaskets emptied and big bags replaced.
  • Dusting, removing cobwebs from ceiling, light fixtures, wall hangings and curtains.
  • Clean up dirty clothes and put them in your washing basket.
  • Beds made.
  • Wipe and dust all surfaces including bedside tables.
  • Mirrors and cabinets wiped, dusted and polished.
  • Blinds wiped down to remove any dust.
  • Rugs vacuumed, floor mopped.

Living Room & Common Areas

Stairs cases banisters dusted and wiped down. Damp dust and polishing of furniture fixtures and fitting, windowsills and skirting boards.

Interior windows cleaned, carpeted areas hoovered, wooden/tiled floors hoovered and mopped.

Extra cleaning services

Commercial cleaning available as is an ironing service.
